Write the SQL DDL to create the following 5 tables describing airports, airplanes, passengers, and flights.
Part 1
An $\textbf{Airplane}$ table where each airplane is identified by a field called $\textbf{id}$ that is a string of up to 10 characters. Other attributes include $\textbf{model}$ (string up to 20 characters), and a $\textbf{manufacture date}$. (1 mark)
Part 2
An $\textbf{Airplane}$ table where each airport has an $\textbf{id}$ of exactly 5 characters, a $\textbf{name}$ (up to 30 characters), and is located in a $\textbf{city}$ (up to 40 characters), a $\textbf{province}$ (up to 20 characters), and a $\textbf{country}$ (up to 20 characters). (1.5 marks)
Part 3
A $\textbf{Flight}$ table where each flight is identified by both a $\textbf{number}$ (exactly 5 characters) and $\textbf{departure date/time}$ (DATETIME). Note that the flight number does not by itself identify a flight as airlines reuse flight numbers. A flight departs from one airport ($\textbf{departAirport}$) and arrives at another ($\textbf{arriveAirport}$). Besides the departure date/time, there is an expected $\textbf{arrival date/time}$, and $\textbf{actual departure time}$ and $\textbf{actual arrival time}$. Each flight record also stores the airplane $\textbf{id}$ of the plane. $\textit{Make all foreign keys set to null on delete and cascade on update}$. (3 marks)
Part 4
A $\textbf{Passenger}$ table where each passenger is identified by an integer $\textbf{id}$. Also store a $\textbf{first name}$ and $\textbf{last name}$ (both up to 30 characters) and a $\textbf{birthdate}$. Other attributes include $\textbf{street}$ (50 chars), $\textbf{city}$ (40 chars), $\textbf{province}$ (20 chars), and $\textbf{country}$ (20 chars). (1.5 marks)
Part 5
An $\textbf{OnFlight}$ table that stores information about passengers on flights. Each record stores the $\textbf{passenger id}$, $\textbf{flight number}$, $\textbf{flight departure date/time}$, and a $\textbf{seat number}$ (exactly 4 characters). $\textit{Make all foreign keys set to perform no action (generate error) on delete and cascade on update}$. (3 marks)
