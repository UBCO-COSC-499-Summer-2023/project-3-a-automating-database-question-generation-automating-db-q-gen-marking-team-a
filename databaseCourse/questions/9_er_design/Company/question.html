Design a database describing companies with locations, employees, and products. A [company](company) is identified by a unique [name](companyName) and also has information on the [industry](industry) the company is in and the [annual revenue](annualRevenue) generated. Companies have [employees](employee) working at them. Each [employee](employee) has an [id](employeeId) as well as a [first name](firstName), [last name](lastName), and [birthdate](birthdate). An [employee](employee) may be [working at](workingAt) multiple companies, and for each company they [work at](workingAt) they have a [salary](salary) and [startDate](startDate). A [company](company) may have zero or more [employees](employee). There is one special employee called the [CEO](ceo). A company has exactly one employee as the [CEO](ceo). An employee can only be the [CEO](ceo) of one company. An [employee](employee) may [manage](manages) multiple other employees but does not have to and may not have a manager. A [company](company) has one or more [locations](location), which are identified on a per company basis by a [location name](locationName). Each [location](location) has an [address](address) and details about its [space](space). An [employee](employee) may be [located at](locatedAt) multiple company [locations](location), and [locations](location) may have multiple [employees](employee) located there. At each particular location, an [employee](employee) may have multiple [room numbers](roomNumber) and a [description](description) of how the room is used. A [company](company) [sells](sells) [products](product). Products are identified by an [id](productId) and also have a [name](productName) and [price](productPrice). [Products](product) belong to exactly one company. At each [company](company) [location](location) there may be an [inventory of products](inventory) of a particular [quantity](quantity).
